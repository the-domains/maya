Abundant Greens 2
Stay in that place

Stay;

be in this place of wonder,

where everything I see

is just for me.

Where rain and thunder

bring,

such a grand energy

to all the grasses

and open fields.

There,

water amasses,

until its power wields

all of life to expand,

right on queue,

and at my command.

Maya
